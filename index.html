Highly Customizable

Relying upon WaveBlocks, our design is completely modular. This enables limitless customization. Since WaveBlocks are very diverse in their technological features, everything from sounds and winning scores to colors and block placement can be customized. This way, every child can find a game to their liking and abilities, preventing the negative emotional impact of not finding suitable games.

Healthy

Given WaveGround's customizability, games can be tailored such that children can develop their desired physical or mental skills in a playful manner. With the interactive features of the WaveBlocks, children won't perceive learning as a burden, but as a fun game.

Interactive

WaveBlocks can emit sounds, vibrate, light up, and change color and shape. All these technologies make for very interactive and engaging games, enhancing the experience of playing.
